HOW TO PLAY

Guess the secret 5-letter word in 6 tries. After each guess, each letter shows whether the answer letter is higher ↑, lower ↓, or exact ✓ in the alphabet.

Example: guessing BLAND when the answer is CROWN:

↑B
↑L
↑A
↑N
↑D

Every letter in CROWN comes after the corresponding letter in BLAND — go higher on all five!
